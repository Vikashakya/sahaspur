About Sahaspur

Sahaspur village is located in Bidhuna tehsil of Auraiya district in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated 5km away from sub-district headquarter Bidhuna (tehsildar office) and 40km away from district headquarter Auraiya. Rathgaon is the gram panchayat of Sahaspur village.

The total geographical area of village is 152.16 hectares. Sahaspur has a total population of 1,235 peoples, out of which male population is 646 while female population is 589. Literacy rate of sahaspur village is 56.84% out of which 61.92% males and 51.27% females are literate. There are about 208 houses in sahaspur village.

Auraiya is nearest town to sahaspur for all major economic activities, which is approximately 120km away.
